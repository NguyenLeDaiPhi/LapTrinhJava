Plan for N4 level

Focus on this path and you will succeed

  • Month 1

    N5 Review and Basic Verb Forms

    Review all N5 grammar, vocabulary, and Kanji. Begin learning essential N4 verb conjugations, focusing on the thể て (te-form) and its related patterns. Learn around 200 new vocabulary words and 50 new Kanji.

  • Month 2

    Expanding Grammar and Complex Sentences

    Focus on more complex verb forms like the Potential Form (可能形), Passive Form (受身形), and Volitional Form (意向形). Learn how to connect clauses to form complex sentences. Add 250 vocabulary words and 60 Kanji.

  • Month 3

    Situational Communication

    Learn grammar patterns used in daily communication, such as asking for advice (～たらいいですか), reporting hearsay (～そうです), and more advanced giving/receiving patterns. Practice reading longer passages and listening to faster-paced conversations. Learn an additional 250 vocabulary words and 65 Kanji.

  • Month 4

    Final Review and N4 Exam Prep

    Review all learned material: approximately 700 new vocabulary words, 175 new Kanji, and all N4 grammar structures. Concentrate on solving N4 mock exams to practice time management and test-taking strategies.
