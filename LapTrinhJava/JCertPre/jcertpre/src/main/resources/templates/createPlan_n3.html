Plan for N3 level

Focus on this path and you will succeed

  • Month 1

    Reinforcement & Intermediate Grammar

    Thoroughly review all N4 knowledge, especially complex verb forms. Begin learning N3 grammar patterns that have similar meanings but different nuances (e.g., ～うちに, ～間に, ～あいだに). Focus on reading short passages and getting used to abstract vocabulary. Learn around 300 new vocabulary words and 80 new Kanji.

  • Month 2

    Complex Sentences and Spoken Language

    Study grammar structures used to connect sentences and express cause-and-effect or complex assumptions (e.g., ～ものだから, ～おかげで, ～せいだ). Start familiarizing yourself with common forms of spoken language, slang, and shortened expressions. Add 350 vocabulary words and 90 Kanji.

  • Month 3

    High-Speed Reading and Listening

    Focus on developing reading comprehension skills with varied topics (articles, emails, announcements). Practice listening to longer conversations at a near-natural speed and grasping the main points. Finish learning the remaining N3 grammar patterns. Learn an additional 350 vocabulary words and 100 Kanji.

  • Month 4

    Exam Practice and Skill Refinement

    Review all material: approximately 1,000 new vocabulary words, 270 new Kanji, and all N3 grammar. Dedicate this month to solving N3 mock exams, carefully analyzing mistakes, especially in tricky areas like homonyms and similar-looking grammar patterns.
