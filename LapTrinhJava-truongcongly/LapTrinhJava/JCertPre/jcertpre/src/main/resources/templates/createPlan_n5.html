Plan for N5 level

Focus on this path and you will succeed

  • Month 1

    Introduction

    Master the Hiragana and Katakana alphabets. Learn basic greetings, fundamental grammar with です/ます, around 100 vocabulary words, and the first 25 Kanji.

  • Month 2

    Sentence Building

    Learn an additional 200 vocabulary words and 35 Kanji. Master grammar for location and time. Start practicing reading short sentences and listening to simple conversations.

  • Month 3

    Basic Communication

    Learn another 250 vocabulary words and the final 40 Kanji. Focus on grammar for expressing wants, giving, and receiving. Practice reading short paragraphs and work on listening comprehension.

  • Month 4

    Final Review and Exam Prep

    Review all 650 vocabulary words, 100 Kanji, and all grammar points. Focus on solving N5 mock exams to get accustomed to the pressure and time constraints
